Low and Slow

Posted by BGB, Novemer 15, 2012

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sousvide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature of the food. In his book, Cooking for Greeks, Jeff Potter describes it as ultra-low-temperaturepoaching.

Next month, we will be serving Sous Vide Salmon with Dill Hollandaise. To reserve a seat at Chef table, contact us before November 30.
